YES-NIGERIA

Youth Entrepreneurial Summit Nigeria

The YES-Nigeria seeks to engage entrepreneurs with 10-20 years more entrepreneurship experience to guide and fast-track the youth into a world that is not forgiving. It's a world that relies upon well thought out decision-making and scraping the depths of your mental well-being to pull you through both the best and the worst times in business. The YES-Nigeria No. 1 tip without a doubt for budding entrepreneurs is to find them direct mentor. Someone you can bounce ideas off, someone who can call you up on business choices and someone who can be there when you need the support the most....

OUR MISSION

Our mandate is to unanimously create a path for better growth amongst the teeming population of enterprising youth in Nigeria

OUR VISION

Is to be the leading Private Sector Group that provides Nigerian young entrepreneurs and its organization with the ability to perform and succeed through mentoring

OUR AGENDA

The Youth Entrepreneurial Summit Nigeria was formed to help bright and young Nigerian youth to become successful entrepreneurs in order to be able to transform from being job seekers to Job owners. We want to promulgate the idea of making young and enterprising Nigerians stay and succeed in their entrepreneurial drive here in Nigeria thereby curbing the loss of our young intelligent Nigerians to other countries in their quest for greener pastures.
